Definitions for the classes

Patient: A patient would be a person who has an appointment with a doctor
for health check up through blood samples.

Blood Sample: A blood sample is when the patient has gone through the process
of extracting blood by a doctor to check for amount of saveral substance
in the patient's blood.

Blood Test: A blood test is a test where the doctors test for amount of substance
in a patient's blood by testing a sample of the blood.
